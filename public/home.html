About

This demo app shows a minimal patient/doctor workflow. Use the registration page to create a patient (stores to JSON demo store), then login as the patient to upload documents.

Developer notes: see `migrations/create_documents.sql` for DB schema and `src/lib/db.js` for metadata persistence.
